## SEZIONI RICHIESTE

### 1. **PROJECT & TEAM SETUP**
- Input: Nome progetto, descrizione, timeline, team size
- Team member management (name, role, capacity hours/day, skills)
- Sprint configuration (durata sprint, giorni lavorativi, team holidays)
- Definisci Agile framework (Scrum, Kanban, Hybrid)
- Store su Google Sheets (sheet: "Projects", "Team Members")

### 2. **PRODUCT BACKLOG MANAGEMENT**
- Crea/Edita/Elimina user stories con template standard:
  - User Story ID
  - Title
  - Description (As a... I want... So that...)
  - Business Value (1-10)
  - Story Points (Fibonacci: 1,2,3,5,8,13,21)
  - Priority (Must/Should/Could/Won't)
  - Status (Backlog/Ready for Sprint/In Sprint/Done)
  - Acceptance Criteria (checklist)
  - Tags (backend, frontend, design, etc.)
  - Dependencies (link a altre stories)
  - Assignee
  - Created Date / Last Updated
- **Export to Google Sheets** (sheet: "Product Backlog")
- **Import from Google Sheets** (read existing stories)
- Bulk action: prioritize, reorder, change status
- Search/filter by priority, tag, status, assignee
- Sorting: drag-and-drop to reorder backlog
- Visual: mostra priorità visivamente (colori)

### 3. **ESTIMATION TOOLS (Multiple Methods)**

#### 3a. **Planning Poker (Fibonacci)**
- Seleziona user story da stimare
- Mostra descrizione, acceptance criteria, dipendenze
- Ogni team member vote silenziosamente (1,2,3,5,8,13,21,?)
- Una volta tutti votato, reveal tutti i voti
- Se tutti uguali → accept. Se diverse → discuss button (mostra outliers, chat per discussione)
- Finalmente → accept & save estimate
- Opzione "skip" se non si sa (?) → team deve ridefinire story

#### 3b. **T-Shirt Sizing**
- Seleziona mode: XS, S, M, L, XL
- Same process: silent vote → reveal → discuss → accept
- Poi convert a story points (XS=1, S=2, M=3, L=5, XL=8, XXL=13)

#### 3c. **Three-Point Estimation (PERT)**
- Per story, team vota:
  - Optimistic (best case)
  - Realistic (most likely)
  - Pessimistic (worst case)
- Auto-calculate: (O + 4*R + P) / 6
- Mostra range: "Questo story è 3-8 punti, probabilmente 5"

#### 3d. **Bucket System / Affinity**
- Mostra numero ordine di stories (randomizzato)
- Team drag-drop stories in buckets: 1pt, 2pt, 3pt, 5pt, 8pt, 13pt
- Più veloce, meno discussione

**Salvare tutte le stime su Google Sheets** (sheet: "Estimations", con history)

### 4. **SPRINT PLANNING**
- Crea/Edita/Elimina sprint (nome, start date, end date, sprint goal)
- **Wizard guidato**:
  1. Seleziona "Ready for Sprint" stories dal backlog
  2. Mostra team velocity (media ultimi 3 sprint)
  3. Calcola: "Capacity = team members × capacity hours/day × days disponibili"
  4. Suggerisci stories fino a riempire capacity (auto-select basato su priority)
  5. Drag-drop per aggiungere/rimuovere stories
  6. Preview: "Sprint ha 45 story points, capacity è 50pt, OK"
  7. Mostra risk: "Hai 3 stories con dipendenze esterne, rischio di blocker"
  8. Aggiungi sprint goal (text field, max 200 char)
  9. **Save to Google Sheets** (sheet: "Sprints")

**Sprint Dashboard**:
- Sprint goal, timeline, status (Planning/Active/Review/Closed)
- Burndown chart real-time (asse X: giorni, asse Y: story points rimanenti)
- Burnup chart (asse X: giorni, asse Y: story points completed)
- Velocity trend (ultimi 5 sprint, chart)
- Team capacity usage (%)
- Risk register (blockers, dependencies, risks)
- Daily standup notes (mini-form: "Ieri [cosa], Oggi [cosa], Blockers [cosa]")

### 5. **BACKLOG REFINEMENT**
- **Wizard guidato per refine stories**:
  1. Seleziona story "To Refine" (status = Backlog, not estimated)
  2. Mostra history (created when, last updated when)
  3. Discuti criteri di accettazione (mostra existing, aggiungi/edita)
  4. Definisci dipendenze (select da altre stories)
  5. Aggiungi task tecnici (sub-tasks dentro la story)
  6. Stima complexity (Low/Medium/High)
  7. Assign team member preliminary (o leave unassigned)
  8. Mark "Ready for Sprint" se refine è completo
  9. **Save to Google Sheets**

### 6. **KANBAN BOARD (Per Sprint In-Progress)**
- Colonne: Backlog, Ready, In Progress, In Review, Done
- Card per ogni user story (ID, title, assignee, points)
- Drag-drop tra colonne (update status)
- Click card → dettagli + subtask checklist + comments
- Real-time estimate of completion (AI-powered): "Basato su velocity, sprint finisce il [date]"
- **Per ogni story: track hours spent** (input field: actual hours today)
- Burndown si aggiorna in real-time
- Visual: color-coded per priority

### 7. **TEAM & RESOURCE MANAGEMENT**
- Mostra team members in sprint (name, role, capacity, hours used today)
- Dashboard: chi è overallocated? Chi ha capacità libera?
- Visualizza skill matrix (chi sa fare cosa)
- Allocazione per story (team member → ore stimate)
- Workload balance: "John 8/8 ore (100%), Sarah 6/8 ore (75%)"
- Suggestion: "Sarah ha 2 ore libere, puoi assegnarle [task]"

### 8. **RETROSPECTIVE & REVIEWS**
- **Sprint Review Form** (post-sprint):
  - Stories completed vs planned
  - Velocity (punti completed)
  - Cosa è andato bene (free text)
  - Cosa è andato male (free text)
  - Action items (next sprint)
  - Demo notes (what we're showing)
- **Sprint Retrospective Form** (post-sprint):
  - Cosa è andato bene (team)
  - Cosa è migliorato (vs sprint precedente)
  - Cosa miglioriamo prossimo sprint (3-5 items)
  - Gladness/Sad/Mad exercise (opzionale: mostra emotions)
  - Velocity trend (compare con sprint precedenti)
  - Team sentiment (1-5 scale)
- **Save to Google Sheets** (sheet: "Retrospectives", "Reviews")

### 9. **METRICS & REPORTS**
- **Dashboard Metriche**:
  - Velocity trend (5 sprint ultimi)
  - Sprint completion rate (%)
  - Story completion rate per team member
  - Story points per priority (Must/Should/Could/Won't)
  - Cycle time (da Backlog a Done, days)
  - Lead time (da Created a Done, days)
  - Cumulative Flow Diagram (Backlog, In Progress, Done over time)
  - Team productivity (points per person)
  - Blockers rate (per sprint)
  - Estimation accuracy (estimated vs actual)

- **Export Reports to Google Sheets**:
  - Sprint summary report (sheet: "Sprint Reports")
  - Team velocity report
  - Risk register
  - Burndown/Burnup data

### 10. **GOOGLE SHEETS INTEGRATION**
- **Export Features**:
  - Export product backlog → Google Sheets
  - Export sprint plan → Google Sheets
  - Export burndown data → Google Sheets (importabile in Excel/Sheets)
  - Export team capacity → Google Sheets
  - Export retrospective notes → Google Sheets

- **Import Features**:
  - Import user stories from Google Sheets (template: ID, Title, Description, Value, Priority, Status)
  - Import team members from Google Sheets (Name, Role, Capacity, Skills)
  - Import project config from Google Sheets
  - Auto-validate (controlla formato, duplicates, required fields)
  - Preview before import

- **Live Sync Option** (opzionale):
  - Se user abilita, app legge Google Sheets ogni X minuti
  - Update app automaticamente se dati sono changed in Sheets
  - Se app è modificata, auto-scrive su Sheets (2-way sync)

- **Authentication**:
  - OAuth2 per Google (user fa login con Google)
  - Salva access token securely
  - Mostra quale Google account è connesso
  - Button: "Disconnect" da Google

## EXAMPLE WORKFLOWS

### Workflow 1: Setup + First Sprint Planning
1. PM crea project (nome "Fashion PMO v2", team size 5)
2. PM importa 3 team members da Google Sheets (John-BE, Sarah-FE, Marco-Design)
3. PM importa 15 user stories da Google Sheets (pre-written backlog)
4. PM stima stories usando Planning Poker (team vote)
5. PM crea Sprint 1 (Jan 15-28), drag-drop stories fino a 40 points
6. PM salva sprint plan to Google Sheets
7. PM comunica sprint goal a team via email (copy-paste dall'app)

### Workflow 2: Daily Sprint Execution
1. Team member accede Kanban board
2. Team member vede "In Progress" stories assigned a lui
3. Team member drag-drop story to "In Review" quando completo
4. Kanban update points remaining (burndown chart aggiorna)
5. PM vede chart, capisce progression
6. Se blocker, team member aggiunge commento su story
7. PM vede risk, resolve

### Workflow 3: Sprint Review + Retro
1. Sprint finisce Friday
2. PM apre "Sprint Review" form
3. PM compila: 12/15 stories completed, velocity 38pt, went well [cosa], went bad [cosa]
4. Team apre "Sprint Retro" form, ognuno compila sentiment
5. PM genera PDF report + salva su Google Sheets
6. PM usa reports per sprint planning successivo (adjust velocity, capacity, ecc)
