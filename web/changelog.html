Release Notes

All the latest updates and improvements to Keisen.

v1.0 Launch January 2026

First public release of Keisen — the all-in-one agile platform for modern teams. Five integrated tools to manage your workflow from idea to delivery.

Smart Todo

  • Kanban board with custom columns, drag & drop, and WIP monitoring
  • Tasks with priority, due dates, subtasks, comments, and file attachments
  • Custom color tags, team assignments, and per-task audit log
  • Global view across all lists with filters (Today, My Tasks, Owner)
  • CFD analytics with bottleneck detection and flow health analysis
  • Archive management and CSV export

Eisenhower Matrix

  • 4-quadrant prioritization with drag & drop reordering
  • Independent voting on urgency and importance with reveal system
  • Three views: grid, scatter chart, and priority list
  • RACI matrix for role assignments (Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed)
  • CSV import/export

Estimation Room

  • 7 estimation modes: Fibonacci, T-Shirt, Decimal, PERT (3-point), Dot Voting, Bucket System, Five Fingers
  • Real-time voting with auto-reveal and statistics (mean, median, std deviation)
  • Story management with session lifecycle (Draft → Active → Completed)
  • Live participant presence with heartbeat tracking

Agile Process Manager

  • Scrum, Kanban, and Hybrid framework support
  • Full sprint lifecycle with backlog, user stories, and story points
  • Configurable Kanban columns with WIP limits
  • Live metrics: velocity, burndown, sprint health, team workload
  • Advanced analytics: scope changes, commitment trend, flow efficiency, blocked items
  • Team roles: Product Owner, Scrum Master, Dev Team

Retrospective Board

  • 6 templates: Start/Stop/Continue, Mad/Sad/Glad, 4Ls, Sailboat, DAKI, Starfish
  • Guided phases: Setup → Icebreaker → Writing → Voting → Discussion → Completed
  • Action items with assignee, priority, due date, and SMART prompts
  • Agile Coach overlay with contextual tips and discussion prompts
  • Sentiment tracking and phase timer

Cross-Tool Integrations

Eisenhower → Smart Todo / Estimation Room / Agile Sprint

Export prioritized activities from any quadrant directly into todo tasks, estimation stories, or sprint user stories — keeping priorities aligned across tools.

Estimation Room → Smart Todo / Agile Sprint

After estimation, push stories with their agreed points into your todo lists or sprint backlog in one click.

Agile Process → Retrospective Board

Link retrospectives to sprints for contextual review. Sprint metrics are available during the retro discussion phase.

Global Dashboard ↔ All Tools

Favorites, deadlines, and pending invites from every tool surface on the home dashboard for a unified overview.

Platform

  • Google Sign-In and email/password authentication
  • 4 languages: English, Italian, Spanish, French
  • Dark, Light, and System theme modes
  • Real-time collaboration with online presence and email invites
  • Responsive design for desktop, tablet, and mobile
  • 3 plans: Free, Premium, Elite
  • GDPR compliance with data export and account deletion
